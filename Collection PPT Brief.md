# Collection PPT Brief

## Audience
CFO / Management

## Purpose
Summarize the accounting cost reduction opportunity, current workload baseline, process concentration, and current action pipeline in a concise management-review format.

## Style
- Minimal wording
- Numbers first
- Conclusion-led
- CFO-oriented
